About Us

IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS) at BMSIT&M is dedicated to advancing technology in AI, ML, and computational intelligence. We aim to foster innovation and provide a platform for students to collaborate and grow.

Through workshops, hackathons, and research initiatives, we empower students to push the boundaries of intelligent systems. Our community-driven approach ensures knowledge sharing and professional development.
